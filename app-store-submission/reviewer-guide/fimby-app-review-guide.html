1 What FIMBY is

FIMBY (Family In My Backyard) is a charity-operated neighbourhood mutual-aid app for adults 19+, run by Strathcona Vineyard Church, a registered Canadian charity in BC. Neighbours ask for and offer help, lend and borrow items, coordinate bulk buys, host gatherings, and message one another — all scoped to a single verified neighbourhood.

It is invite-only and neighbourhood-scoped: a normal account only ever sees its own neighbourhood. So you can review the full experience without involving real neighbours, we have pre-seeded a dedicated "Reviewer's Neighbourhood" with posts, library items, a conversation, and a pre-vouched account.

Native iOS features: Apple Push Notifications for neighbour messages and lending reminders, secure on-device sign-in with credentials stored in the iOS Keychain, Universal Links for the sign-in callback, and per-user quiet hours that silence alerts on-device. The app is a full mutual-aid client — asks, offers, a lending library with due-date tracking, bulk-buy coordination, events, and direct & group messaging.

2 Guided test path

Everything below is reachable from the bottom navigation after sign-in.

  1. Sign in with the demo account. You land on the neighbourhood Home feed (a time-of-day greeting, e.g. "Good afternoon, …").
  2. Create a post — tap the centre ＋ button → choose an Ask, Offer, or Shared Life post → submit. It appears in the feed.
  3. Respond / message — open any feed card, respond, and exchange messages. Threads live under the Messages tab.
  4. Library & lending — open the Library tab to browse items and send a borrow request on one.
  5. Notifications — tap the bell (top-right) to see in-app notifications; push arrives on a physical device.
  6. Account deletion — My Stuff → Settings → Delete my account (in-app, Guideline 5.1.1(v)). Also documented at fimby.com/delete-account.

3 Safety & moderation (Guideline 1.2)

FIMBY meets all four UGC requirements. Tools are reachable on every surface where content appears:

✓Report objectionable contentOn every feed card & detail page (Asks/Offers, Shared Life, Library, Loaned items) and inside message & response threads.
✓Block abusive usersIn direct-message threads, response threads, and on neighbour profiles. Blocks are bidirectional.
✓Filter & act within 24hA binding EULA prohibits objectionable content; every report is reviewed within 24 hours by a neighbourhood moderator, FIMBY team as backup.
✓Published contacthelp@fimby.com, plus the full policy at fimby.com/community-standards.

Reporter identity is confidential by default. A dispute or report is never assigned to a party involved in it. The full ladder of responses (content removal → warning → restriction → removal) is published in our Community Standards.

4 Context that helps review

Age requirement (19+)
BC's age of majority is 19. The App Store ceiling is 18+, so 19+ eligibility is enforced via a required age attestation at onboarding and in our Terms (§2 "Who can use FIMBY"). The store rating is set to 18+.
Neighbourhood scoping
All content is filtered to the signed-in user's neighbourhood. The seeded Reviewer's Neighbourhood exists so you see a populated, self-contained community without contacting real members.
Acting-as identity
A neighbour may help someone else (e.g. a support person posting for a senior). The app always shows who an action is "posted as" / "messaging as". The demo account acts as itself.
Fully functional
All services are live and accessible during review. Every feature works end-to-end with real data in the Reviewer's Neighbourhood — there is no demo-mode stubbing.
