# Strategic North Star (internal — NOT visitor copy)

Implementer compass for fimby.com copy and structure. Do not paste this language onto public pages.

## Thesis

FIMBY is **local social resilience infrastructure** — a *build trust before you need it* platform. Surface what already exists in the product and site; do not invent fear-driven framing.

## Emphasis rules

- **Resilience through relationship** — thicker local trust, built in ordinary times.
- **Measure trust, not engagement** — belonging over performance as a metrics rule.
- **Make receiving as normal as giving** — dignity over efficiency.
- **Scale like yeast, not a social network** — depth in recognizable cells over reach.

## Anti-fear guardrail

No AI-disruption, collapse, or prepper framing in public copy. Public lane: *life is changing fast, local trust matters, start with the people close enough to actually show up* — ordinary-first, hopeful, never apocalyptic.

## Success metrics (trust, not vanity)

Track:

- V6A funnel: homepage → sign-up starts → completed onboarding
- Expansion funnel: contact/waitlist with "help launch" checked → reply rate
- **Trust indicators:** repeat asks/offers without prompting, known-neighbour connections, people willing to host/coordinate, qualitative "I know more neighbours now"
- Diagnostic only: scroll depth to capability strip; time on how-it-works summary

Do **not** optimize for sign-up volume at the expense of recognizability.
