### Description (paste)

```
FIMBY (Family In My Backyard) is a neighbourhood mutual-aid platform for the people who actually live near you — ask for help, offer what you have, lend and borrow, gather, and share life in small everyday ways. Not a city-wide feed. Just the people around you.

FIMBY works in any browser at our.fimby.com and as a free app for iPhone and Android — same account, same neighbourhood.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Post asks and offers for everyday help
• Borrow and lend tools, books, and gear through the Library
• RSVP to gatherings and neighbourhood events
• Share stories and thank-yous
• Message neighbours privately
• Coordinate bulk buys with people nearby

BUILT FOR REAL NEIGHBOURHOODS
FIMBY is scoped to your immediate area — the same faces, not thousands of strangers. No ads, no algorithm, no data sales. Free to use.

TRUST BY DESIGN
Before borrowing items, a neighbour who knows you vouches for you — the kind of introduction communities have always made. Block anyone anytime. You control what contact info you share.

WHO IT'S FOR
Anyone 19+ in an active FIMBY neighbourhood. Currently live in Vancouver's V6A (Strathcona, Chinatown, Downtown Eastside). Sign up with your postal code to join or get on the waitlist.

FIMBY is a non-profit endeavour supported by Strathcona Vineyard Church. Learn more at fimby.com.
```
